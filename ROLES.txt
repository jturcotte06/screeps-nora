** ROLES OUTLINE **

MINER: MINE SOURCE -> DEPOSIT ENERGY INTO CONTAINER/LINK/GROUND

CLEANER: PICKUP RESOURCES -> DEPOSIT INTO STORAGE AREA

HAULER: COLLECT RESOURCES FROM MINER CONTAINERS -> DEPOSIT INTO STORAGE AREA

SUPPLIER: COLLECT ENERGY FROM STORAGE AREA -> SUPPLY SPAWNS/EXTENSIONS/TOWERS/UPGRADE CONTAINERS
